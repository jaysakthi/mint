Our Story

Born from a Love
of Earth's Treasures

Mintakas began with a simple conviction: the most beautiful jewelry doesn't need embellishment — it needs authenticity. Named after one of the three stars of Orion's Belt, we believe every gemstone carries millions of years of earth's story within it.

As a women-owned studio, we pour intention into every piece. From sourcing genuine gemstones to hand-finishing each setting, our process honors both the material and the wearer. We create jewelry that doesn't just adorn — it connects you to something ancient and enduring.
